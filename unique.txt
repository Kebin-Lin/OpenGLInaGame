Rules/Controls:
Press UP or DOWN to move lanes.
Don't touch the takodachis running at you.
Takodachis speed up and become more frequent as the game goes on.

Unique Features:
- Enemies
- Varying velocities
- Difficulty increase as time goes on
- Animation
- Text rendering
- Game states (GAME_OVER or RUNNING)
- Option to replay on game over to reset score and difficulty
